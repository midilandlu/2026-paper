## Translation Challenges
- **Academic syntax**: The paper uses complex, nested sentence structures typical of academic writing. These must be broken down to ensure a natural flow in Traditional Chinese without losing logical precision.
- **Interdisciplinary jargon**: The text draws from cognitive psychology and HCI. Standardized translations for these fields (e.g., dual-process theory, heuristic cues) must be used.
